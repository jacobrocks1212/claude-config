# Cognito Forms Payments Skill

This skill provides expert knowledge on the payment processing system in Cognito Forms. Use this skill when working on payment provider integrations, debugging payment-related issues, or modifying the payment flow during entry submission.

## When to Reference Additional Files

-   To understand the high-level design and the roles of different services, read **`architecture-overview.md`**.
-   For specific implementation details about Stripe, PayPal, or Square, refer to **`provider-guides.md`**.
-   To understand the step-by-step process a payment goes through during entry submission, read **`payment-flow.md`**.
